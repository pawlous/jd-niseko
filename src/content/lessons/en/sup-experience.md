Meeting point: Lake Toya — small park parking lot; Niseko — drop-in point specified in your pre-trip notice.

Lesson time: AM 10:00 – PM 12:00, about 2 hours total (subject to weather and water conditions on the day).

Group size: max 6 people.

## What's included in the experience
🔹 Beginner (Lake Toya): gear intro, on-land instruction, standing practice, lake cruising, photos
🔹 Advanced (Niseko Shiribetsu River): advanced paddling techniques, moving-water riding, photos

Language: Mandarin (Japanese also available).

## What's included (fees)
⭕ Included: guide, SUP gear and life jacket rental, basic photography service
❌ Not included: transfers, meals, personal insurance, other personal expenses (waterproof phone pouch rental available on site)

## Notes
⚠️ Under-18s need guardian consent before entering the water.
⚠️ Minimum age 7; ages 7–11 are individually assessed for riding tandem with an adult.
⚠️ Maximum age 65.
❌ Pregnant guests or those with certain medical conditions cannot join.
