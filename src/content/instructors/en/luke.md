Hi, I'm Luke — ever since I landed my first S-turn in life, I haven't been able to stop.

I've ridden Nagano, Echigo-Yuzawa, Gunma, Tohoku, and of course every corner of Hokkaido's resorts, big and small.

I like teaching my fellow riders with patience and a relaxed, fun approach. From cruising green runs to catching air off a jump — wherever it is, as long as I'm riding free, that's my favorite style.
