Born in sun-baked southern Taiwan, Dante dropped his job back home on a whim to come ski in Hokkaido — and after one run, he was hooked, vowing to share the thrill of riding snow with as many people as possible. Based across Niseko United, he teaches both ski and snowboard with a style that's clear, easy to follow, and full of humor (with the occasional teasing). Whether you're shy or outgoing, you'll feel at home in Dante's lessons.

In summer, he's an amateur farmer, traveler, and YouTuber. If life brings you to Hokkaido, let him take a little of your time to experience the colorful side of this land together.
