I'm Jason, from Yilan, Taiwan. I've been into skiing for five years now — my love for the sport led me to get certified as a Canadian snowboard instructor, and I decided to teach skiing in Hokkaido.

I've ridden resorts in France, Andorra, and Japan, but Hokkaido is absolutely my favorite region — beyond the high-quality powder, there's endless great food and relaxing hot springs.

I hope to share Hokkaido's beauty and the joy of skiing with more friends!
