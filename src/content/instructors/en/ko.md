Hello! I'm KO, heading into my 3rd season in Niseko.

I lived in Niseko for a full year during a working holiday, and after going home I decided I had to come back to this beautiful little town and become an instructor!

Before skiing, I wasn't really an athletic person, so I deeply understand the frustration and fear beginners feel when their body won't cooperate. That's exactly why I want to help you avoid the rough patches I went through. Don't worry — leave it to me! We'll build your confidence step by step with a systematic teaching approach.

I want every student to master skiing skills in a safe, efficient environment, and truly feel the joy of riding!
